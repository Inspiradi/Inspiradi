      Frequently Asked Questions

      Answers to common questions about Inspiradi.

      What is Inspiradi?

      Inspiradi is a creative tool designed to help artists, writers, and creators of all kinds overcome creative blocks. It uses a combination of curated lists and generative AI to provide unique and engaging sparks of inspiration.

      Is this app free to use? Are there any limits?

      Yes, Inspiradi is free to use! The AI-powered features in The Spark Forge ("Elaborate" and "Get Brief") are unlimited. To ensure the service remains available for everyone, the "Generate Random Brief" feature has a limit of 10 uses per day. This count resets every day.

      Do you claim any rights to the art I create?

      Absolutely not. You, the artist, retain 100% ownership and all rights to your original artwork. Inspiradi is simply a tool to provide a starting idea. The unique creative expression is entirely yours, and we claim no ownership over it. For more details, please see our Terms of Service.

      How is my data handled? What do you save?

      Inspiradi is designed with privacy in mind. All your "Saved Sparks" and folder information are stored directly in your own web browser's `localStorage`. This data is not sent to our servers, and we cannot see or access your saved ideas. If you clear your browser data, your saved sparks will be deleted.

      Are my prompts used to train the AI?

      No. According to Google's API policies, data sent to the Gemini API is not used for training their models. The prompts you send for elaboration or brief generation are processed for the one-time response and are not stored or used for any other purpose.
